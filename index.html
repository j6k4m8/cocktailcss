Irish Godson

A cappuccino with a twist, served cold.

  • One count of Canadian Club whisky and a splash of Amaretto added to 5oz of Counter Culture Apollo cold-brew coffee.
  • Blend 3oz heavy whipping cream with one egg-white, 1.5oz sugar, and 1oz gin. Pour out the liquid and add foam over the coffee.

Mango-ld Fashioned

A New Fashioned. See also: Mango-hattan.

  • Two counts Corner Creek Bourbon whisky with a dash of Angostura bitters.
  • Blend with a handful of frozen mango until smooth. Garnish with lime.
